Incepta Analytics — New York, New York — June 2023 to present.

I'm a Forward Deployed Engineer. My account is New Private Equity, a private
equity client whose data lives in Palantir Foundry. The role is the engineer
end of "tools that ship to people who actually have to act on the output."

I built and maintain a stack of Transforms code repositories in Foundry —
PySpark ETL modules that process CRM data and weekly engagement signals.
After tightening the contracts and adding incremental snapshots, downstream
data errors dropped ~50% and end-to-end runtimes went down ~30%, unlocking
near real-time data availability for the Sales and Finance teams.

On the model side I prototyped and deployed AI models inside Foundry Code
Workspaces: PyTorch churn-prediction models, fine-tuned LLMs, and semantic
search pipelines. I integrated those into the Model Catalog and Ontology so
they're reusable across decision-support apps instead of being one-off
notebooks.

The newest piece is AI Agents in Foundry — autonomous copilots for
leadership workflows. They combine retrieval-augmented generation,
ontology-aware reasoning, and risk classification to auto-summarize
initiatives, detect churn signals, and drive Decision Board accountability.
That cut executive prep cycles by roughly 80%. The eval harness underneath
those agents is, honestly, the part of the work I'm most proud of.

What I like about FDE work: the agents either help the team or they don't,
and you find out within a week of shipping. There's nowhere for a bad demo to
hide.
